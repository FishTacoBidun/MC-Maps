About The Map

Relive is a Minecraft adventure map based on the game Observation Duty. The goal of the game is to traverse through different floors using an elevator and escape from floor 100. Each floor has a chance to contain 1 of 60 different anomalies, some subtle, some deadly. The first time you enter floor 1, it won't have an anomaly, so you know what to look for going forward. Every 10 floors, you will receive a checkpoint. Correctly guessing whether there’s an anomaly will send you up a floor, while incorrectly guessing whether there’s an anomaly or dying will send you back to your most recent checkpoint. Can you escape floor 100?

About The Map

Five Nights at Freddy's Reimagined is a take on the original game with new and unique mechanics inside of Minecraft. It comes with jump scares, cutscenes, 6 animatronics, and 7 different nights to beat with varying difficulty, along with a custom AI mode to custom-make your own challenge. Can you survive all the nights?
